Modal

Introduction

With Powertrain you can enable your specific content to popup over your regular content while disabling the rest of your webpage.
This ensures that your user has undivided attention to the current content being displayed, while also providing multiple ways to interact as well.
To create your modal, ensure to wrap the parent element in .modal-container class, and place the footer using .modal-footer class.
