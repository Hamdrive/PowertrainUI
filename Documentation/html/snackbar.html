Snackbar

Introduction

Snackbars provide brief messages about various processes at the bottom of the screen. These can range from simple messages to also having trigger buttons.
The snackbars provided by PowertrainUI are responsive as well.

Types of Snackbar

There are two types of snackbars available with PowertrainUI: baseline & leading .
By default, the snackbars live at the bottom of the website.
Based on your requirement, you may position the snackbar either at the center or on the leftmost side of the screen using btn-baseline or btn-leading IDs respectively inside your buttons.
